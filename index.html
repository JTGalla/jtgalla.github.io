WORK

  • Discover

    The Discovery phase consists of your inquiry for a website. Introductions will be made, questions will be asked, and we'll get started on talking about what you want in a website. It's nice to meet you!
  • Discuss

    The Discuss phase consists of us spending careful planning on how to best move forward with developing your website. Design and art will be decided then approved by you, and any other requests will be made.
  • Develop

    The Develop phase consists of me programming your website. Multiple updates will be given and tweaks will be made. The final website will then be approved by you. Just sit back and enjoy the ride, you're almost done!
  • Display

    The Display phase consists of that sweet sweet relief of knowing that your website is up and ready to be seen. Final changes will be made and the website published. Ask any questions you might have. Be proud! I sure am.
